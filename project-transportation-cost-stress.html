Project Overview

About This Study

Transportation costs are a growing source of economic pressure for commuters and taxi drivers in Ogun State. Rising fuel prices, fare instability, and infrastructure constraints are reshaping daily mobility and household finances.

This project documents how transport cost stress affects income, access to work, and daily living. Field researchers conducted structured interviews across 12 local government areas between March and May 2026.

"7 out of 10 households now spend more than 20% of monthly income on transportation alone."
