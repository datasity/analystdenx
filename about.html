Our Mission

Turning analysts into contributors through real-world research

Analyst Den is the applied research platform of Datavasity — built to bridge the gap between learning data skills and solving real problems with them. We conduct field research across Ogun State, Nigeria, and publish public-interest insights that policymakers, businesses, and communities can act on.

Every project is a learning environment. Every contributor earns a public credit. Every report is free to access.
